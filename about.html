About #LEARN;
Education Department of WEBbuilders.lk

#LEARN is the official education department of WEBbuilders.lk, a leading Sri Lankan web development brand under Yarl Tech Solution (PVT) Ltd. Launched in November 2025, #LEARN focuses on real-time, practical learning through both free and paid online courses designed for aspiring developers and tech enthusiasts.

Contact Now

Build Up Your Career with #LEARN

Our mission is to bridge education with real industry experience, helping students gain hands-on skills used by professionals at WEBbuilders.lk. Top-performing learners also receive opportunities for paid, project-based internships within WEBbuilders.lk turning learning into real career growth. At #LEARN, we don’t just teach. We build your skills, your confidence, and your future.

Services provided by #LEARN

Our Focus

At #LEARN, we believe education should go beyond theory. Our programs are 100% practical and project-based, giving students real-time work experience from the very beginning. We offer both free and paid online courses that cover a wide range of modern web technologies, tools, and workflows used by professionals at WEBbuilders.lk.

Internship Opportunities

We don’t just teach, we create real career paths. Students who show outstanding performance and creativity are offered paid, project-based internships directly with WEBbuilders.lk. This provides valuable exposure to live client projects, teamwork, and the professional standards of a leading Sri Lankan tech company.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka’s most trusted real-time, industry-connected education platform, helping passionate learners grow into confident web professionals ready for global opportunities.

Powered by Yarl Tech Solution (PVT) Ltd

As part of Yarl Tech Solution (PVT) Ltd, #LEARN carries forward the company’s commitment to innovation, excellence, and community growth. Together with WEBbuilders.lk, we aim to build skills, build experience, and build your future.
